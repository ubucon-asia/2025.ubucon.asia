## **Getting around in Kathmandu**
You can travel inside the valley using:

- **Tempo:** Negotiate fare beforehand
- **Taxis:** Book through apps like Pathao or Indrive.
- **City buses:** Pay fare directly on boarding.

## **Recommended attractions to visit**

- **Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple)**: Iconic stupa atop a hill offering panoramic views of Kathmandu, surrounded by prayer wheels and monkeys. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/28Q9UixFx4M69LDa6)

- **Boudhanath Stupa**: One of the largest spherical stupas in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its serene ambiance. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/EdXQxaeXMRS8Lb2X8)

- **Pashupatinath Temple**: Sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the banks of the Bagmati River. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/b3nawYZukS9S2NKGA)

- **Kathmandu Durbar Square**: Historical royal palace complex featuring traditional Newari architecture, temples, and courtyards. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PmpxA9Qh7iPVh5pv9)

- **Patan Durbar Square**: UNESCO World Heritage Site showcasing ancient palaces, intricate wood carvings, and cultural museums. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/f2Lvb8o8gnn2NbAh8)

- **Bhaktapur Durbar Square**: Well-preserved medieval city square, renowned for its traditional pottery, architecture, and vibrant festivals. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/nQyCkMcWxqutefoa9)

- **Thamel**: Bustling tourist hub with vibrant markets, restaurants, and cultural shops for souvenirs and local cuisine. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/3vdnRFxHTQSfu91b7)

- **Narayanhiti Palace Museum**: Former royal palace turned museum, displaying artifacts and glimpses of Nepal's monarchy. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/3zUfB4F4w9x2awoW7)

These attractions capture the essence of  Kathmandu’s rich history, architecture, and culture.

## **Recommended food and drink**

**Food:**
1. **Thamel House Restaurant**: Traditional Nepali cuisine including Dal Bhat, Momos, and Newari Khaja Set. [(Location)](https://goo.gl/maps/WKg8AXE3WyP2)
     
2. **Bhanchha Ghar**: Authentic Newari dishes like Chatamari, Yomari, and Juju Dhau.[(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qBXVdSyxPwFdWNE8A)
     
3. **Yangling Tibetan Restaurant**: Tibetan specialties such as Thukpa and steamed Momos [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/B3FBEEhjnUdjYymz9)
     
4. **Roadhouse Café**: Variety of pizzas, including Nepali-style options, and international fusion dishes. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/FLzm1nLqzWus3BKq5)

5. **Shandar Momo**: This is a chain restaurant featuring the most popular Nepalese delicacy, Momo. If you want a fulfilling taste, do visit the chain located in Baneshwor. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9uTX3enq6Xow1x4R6)
     
6. **Himalayan Java Coffee**: Variety of coffees and drinks along with tasty breakfast. [(Location)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PQFQ2FHAN7Xyq2zt8)

**Drinks:**
1. **Matka Chai**: Spiced tea with milk, infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and ginger. Search Chiya in google maps, whichever pops up first will surely provide you a flavourful experience.  Location: [Chiya Ghar](https://maps.app.goo.gl/P2VfQiZ3Lkzcdak28)

2. **Lassi**: Refreshing yogurt-based drink, served sweet or salty, perfect for cooling down on hot days. Location: [Indra Chowk Lassi Pasal](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ayA6Ung5x2XJQSpW6)
